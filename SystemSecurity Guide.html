System Security Guide

System Security ensures your computer stays safe from hackers, malware, and unauthorized access. A secure system prevents data theft, privacy leaks, ransomware attacks, and performance damage.

1. Keep Your System Updated

Updates fix vulnerabilities that attackers often exploit. Always keep Windows, macOS, or Linux fully updated.

2. Antivirus & Firewall

Use trusted antivirus software. The firewall blocks unauthorized traffic trying to enter your system.

3. Avoid Pirated Software

90% pirated software contains hidden trojans or keyloggers. Use official websites only.

4. Beware of Suspicious Files

Do not open unknown email attachments, cracked games, or random ZIP/RAR files.
