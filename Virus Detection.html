Virus Detection Guide

Viruses, trojans, ransomware and other malware can steal data, encrypt files, slow your computer and let attackers control your device. Early detection protects your data and limits damage.

1. Infection Signs

  • Slow performance
  • Frequent crashes
  • Unexpected pop-ups
  • New toolbars
  • Unknown apps

2. Immediate Actions

  • Disconnect Internet
  • Check Task Manager
  • Note filenames
  • Avoid entering passwords

3. Use Trusted Scanners

Run a full system scan using up-to-date antivirus tools.
