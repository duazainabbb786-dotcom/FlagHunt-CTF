Password Safety Guide

Principles of strong passwords and secure management. Your password is the first line of defense.

Strong Passwords

  • 12+ characters long
  • Unique for every account
  • No personal information
  • Mix symbols and numbers

Management & MFA

  • Use a Password Manager
  • Enable 2FA/MFA always
  • Check for breaches regularly
